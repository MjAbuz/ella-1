Who am I

bulgarian girl, programmer, dreamer

Hello! My name is Mirela Damyanova. I'm from Bulgaria, born on 25 July 1995. I have passion about computers since I know myself. I owned my first computer in 2000, when I was 5. This is how it all started. I mostly enjoyed playing games, when in 2009 I had my first connection at home to the World Wide Web. Then I spend a couple of endless nights and nerves, writing and learning HTML.

I finished high school in 2014. Right after that I started studying Psychology in University of Veliko Turnovo St Cyril and St. Methodius. But after a year, I found Software University and I decided to give it a try. In August 2015 I pass the entrance exam and I began a part from the coolest community I've ever known. I canceled my studying at Veliko Turnovo in 2017.

And now, after three years studying programming, this is who I am. One ordinary girl, with big ambitious to be a better person. C#, Liverpool FC fan and cats lover. I also like to go out with friends, take one (two, three...) beers, chill or have a really crazy adventures. So this is me. I'm happy to meet you!
